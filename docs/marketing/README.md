## Posting tips

- **LinkedIn:** Tue–Thu, 8–10 AM or 12 PM in your target timezone. Tag industry hashtags (#PromotionalProducts, #B2BSaaS).
- **Facebook:** Wed–Fri, 1–3 PM. Use questions in captions to drive comments.
- **Instagram:** Mon/Wed/Fri, 11 AM–1 PM. Pair every post with a strong visual (screenshot, carousel, or short reel).
- Rotate posts over 3–4 weeks. Don't post all 5 on one platform in a single day.
- Always add your live demo URL before publishing.

## Brand voice

- Confident but not hype-heavy
- Speak to operators who live in spreadsheets, email threads, and Alibaba tabs
- Emphasize **time saved**, **fewer missed deals**, and **professional client experience**
