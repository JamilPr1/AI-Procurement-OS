# 15-Minute Demo Call Script

**Before the call:** Open these tabs:
1. https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/store?tenant=demo
2. https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/#stores (partner stores)
3. https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/marketing (pricing)
4. CRM login (only if doing full walkthrough)

**Goal:** Get them to say "Can I try a pilot?" — not explain every feature.

---

## Minute 0–2: Rapport + discovery

**You say:**
> "Thanks for jumping on. I'll keep this to 15 minutes — mostly show you the product, but first: how are you handling sourcing and client quotes today?"

**Listen for pain:**
- Email chaos / lost RFQs
- Rebuilding proposals every time
- Clients asking for online quote options
- Multiple brands to manage

**You say:**
> "Got it. That's exactly what we built this for. Let me show you — I'll share my screen."

---

## Minute 2–7: Demo store (the hook)

**Open:** https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/store?tenant=demo

**You say:**
> "This is what your **clients** would see — a branded Product Finder store. No static catalog. They describe what they need."

**Do live:**
1. Click a featured product OR type a quote request
2. Walk through 2–3 intake questions (quantity, logo, delivery)
3. Show how it feels like ecommerce, not a contact form

**You say:**
> "Every quote request lands in your CRM pipeline. You're not copying from a website form into a spreadsheet."

**Show partner stores (30 sec):**
> "Same platform, different brands — watch."

Open 1–2 from https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/#stores:
- Promo Pros (drinkware)
- Gift Hub (corporate gifts)
- Event Swag (trade show)

**You say:**
> "Each partner gets their own logo, colors, and product specialties. You provision this from admin — no developer needed."

---

## Minute 7–11: CRM pipeline (if they're engaged)

**Only if they ask "what happens after the quote?" or you have time.**

**You say:**
> "Behind the store is a full sourcing CRM — 11 steps from buyer research to delivery."

**Highlight (don't demo everything):**
- Hot Leads wizard — guided next steps
- Supplier matching + RFQ + quote comparison
- AI drafts outreach and proposals — **you approve before anything sends**
- Deal tracking — proposals sent, revenue, active orders

**You say:**
> "AI does the drafting. Your team keeps control. Nothing goes to a client or factory without your approval."

---

## Minute 11–13: Pricing + pilot offer

**Open:** https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/marketing

**You say:**
> "Two plans: Starter at $99/month for small teams, Growth at $299 for higher volume. Both include the branded store and full CRM."

**Pilot close (beginner offer):**
> "For early partners, I'm offering a **14-day free pilot** — I'll set up your branded store and CRM, walk you through onboarding, no credit card. After 2 weeks, you decide if it's worth $99/month."

---

## Minute 13–15: Next steps + objection handling

**You say:**
> "What would you need to see to try the pilot?"

### Common objections

| They say | You say |
|----------|---------|
| "We already use a CRM" | "Is it built for factory RFQs and promo products? Most teams still do sourcing in email even with Salesforce." |
| "We don't need AI" | "AI only drafts — you approve everything. The win is one system, not autopilot." |
| "Too expensive" | "One saved deal pays for a year. Starter is $99 — less than one hour of ops time per month." |
| "Need to think about it" | "Totally fair. I'll email you the demo store link. Can we book 15 min next Friday to debrief?" |
| "We need custom features" | "Let's start with the pilot and note your top 3 requests. Founding partners shape the roadmap." |

**End with:**
> "I'll send a follow-up email today with the demo link and pilot details. What's the best email for your store setup?"

---

## After the call (within 2 hours)

Send Template D from `outreach-templates.md`.

Log in `lead-tracker.csv`:
- Demo date
- Pain points they mentioned
- Pilot offered? Y/N
- Follow-up date

## If the call goes great (upgrade path)

1. Get company name + desired store branding (logo, colors, specialty)
2. Create tenant in platform admin within 24 hours
3. Send credentials + their store URL: `https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/store?tenant=their-slug`
4. Schedule 30-min onboarding for Day 3 of pilot
5. Check in on Day 7 and Day 13 of pilot
