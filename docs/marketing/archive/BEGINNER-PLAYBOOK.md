# Beginner Marketing Playbook — Get Your First Clients

**Live product:** https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com  
**Your job:** Talk to 100 people. Close 3–5.  
**Budget needed to start:** $0 (organic only for first 30 days)

---

## What you're selling (say this in 15 seconds)

> "AI Procurement OS is a white-label sourcing platform for promotional product distributors and agencies. Your team goes from buyer research to factory quotes to client proposals in one system — and each client gets their own branded online quote store."

**Proof they can touch right now (no login):**
- Demo store → https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/store?tenant=demo
- Pricing → https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/marketing
- Partner stores → https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/#stores

---

## Who to target first (easiest wins)

| Priority | Who | Why they'll care |
|----------|-----|------------------|
| 🥇 1 | Solo promo distributors (1–5 people) | Drowning in email + spreadsheets |
| 🥇 2 | Small marketing agencies that source merch | Want to look bigger to clients |
| 🥈 3 | Corporate gifting startups | Need a quote store + CRM fast |
| 🥈 4 | Event swag freelancers | High volume, tight deadlines |
| 🥉 5 | Mid-size distributors | Longer sales cycle, bigger deals |

**Don't target yet:** Enterprise distributors, companies with custom ERP, anyone who doesn't source promo products.

---

## Your 3 offers (keep it simple)

| Offer | Price | For whom |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Starter** | $99/mo | 1–3 users, 10 deals, branded store |
| **Growth** | $299/mo | Growing teams, 50 deals, priority search |
| **Pilot** | Free 14 days | First 5 prospects you talk to — use this to remove friction |

> **Beginner tip:** Lead with the **free pilot**, not the price. Price comes after they see the demo store.

## Week 1 — Setup (2–3 hours total)

### Day 1: Fix your "storefront"
- [ ] Open your live site on phone + laptop — does it load fast?
- [ ] Send yourself the demo store link — would you buy from this?
- [ ] Set your contact email in Render env: `CONTACT_EMAIL=you@yourdomain.com`
- [ ] Test the "Request demo" form on the homepage — confirm you receive the lead

### Day 2: Write your one-liner everywhere
Use this bio on LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram:

```
Helping promo distributors source faster with AI | 
White-label sourcing platform + branded quote stores | 
Try demo → ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/store?tenant=demo
```

### Day 3: Build a list of 50 prospects
Use `where-to-find-clients.md` + `lead-tracker.csv`. Goal: 50 names with email or LinkedIn.

### Day 4–5: Send 10 outreach messages per day
Use templates in `outreach-templates.md`. Personalize the first line only.

### Day 6–7: Post on 1 social platform
Pick **LinkedIn** if B2B, **Facebook** if you know promo groups. Use Post 1 from `linkedin-posts.md`.

---

## Week 2 — Conversations (goal: 5 demo calls)

| Day | Action | Target |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Mon | 10 cold emails/DMs | 2 replies |
| Tue | Follow up everyone who didn't reply | 1 reply |
| Wed | 10 new outreaches + 1 LinkedIn post | 2 replies |
| Thu | Demo calls (15 min each) | 2 calls booked |
| Fri | Send "here's your demo store" to warm leads | 1 pilot signup |

**Demo call flow:** See `demo-call-script.md` (15 minutes, no slides needed — share screen on live product).

---

## Week 3 — Proof & urgency

- Post a **before/after** story (even if it's hypothetical): "6 tabs → 1 platform"
- Ask 1 pilot user: "Can I quote you?" — use their words in your next post
- Offer **"Founding partner"** pricing: $79/mo Starter locked for 12 months (first 10 clients only)
- Join 2 Facebook/LinkedIn promo product groups — answer questions, don't spam

---

## Week 4 — Scale what worked

- Double down on the channel that got replies (email vs LinkedIn vs Facebook)
- Post 3× per week on your best platform (use `30-DAY-ACTION-PLAN.md`)
- Ask every pilot user for 1 referral
- Goal by end of month: **3 paying clients** or **10 active pilots**

---

## Daily routine (30 min/day minimum)

| Time | Task |
|------|------|
| 10 min | Send 3 personalized outreach messages |
| 10 min | Follow up 2 old leads |
| 10 min | Engage on social (comment, don't just post) |

**Weekly (1 hour):** Write 1 post, review `lead-tracker.csv`, book demos.

---

## What NOT to do as a beginner

❌ Spend money on ads before you have 5 manual conversations  
❌ Build new features instead of selling what exists  
❌ Post without a link to the demo store  
❌ Pitch "AI platform" — pitch "stop losing RFQs in email"  
❌ Give up before 100 outreaches (most replies come after follow-up #2)

## First client checklist (when someone says yes)

1. Create their tenant in platform admin
2. Set up their branded store (logo, colors, specialties)
3. Send login credentials privately (never in public posts)
4. Schedule 30-min onboarding call
5. Ask for testimonial after 2 weeks

**You got this.** The product is live. Now it's a numbers game: outreach → demo → pilot → paid.
