## Personalization cheat sheet (first line only)

Pick one and fill in:

| Their signal | Your opener |
|--------------|-------------|
| Posts about trade shows | "Saw your post about [event] — event swag sourcing is exactly what we built for." |
| Company sells drinkware | "Noticed [Company] focuses on drinkware — our demo store has a full mug/tumbler quote flow." |
| Small team (LinkedIn shows 2–10) | "Looks like a lean team — our Starter plan is built for ops that wear multiple hats." |
| Agency (not distributor) | "As an agency sourcing for clients, the white-label store per brand might be the biggest win." |
| Recent job change | "Congrats on the new role at [Company] — often a good time to modernize sourcing tools." |

## Tracking your outreach

Log every send in `lead-tracker.csv`:
- Date sent
- Template used (A, B, C…)
- Reply? Y/N
- Demo booked? Y/N
- Status (cold / warm / pilot / paid)

**Rule:** Never send the same person the same template twice. Follow up with C, then a custom note.
