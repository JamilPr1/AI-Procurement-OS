# 30-Day Marketing Action Plan

**Live URL:** https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com  
**Monthly goal:** 100 outreaches → 15 replies → 8 demos → 3 paying clients

Mark each day ✅ as you complete it.

---

## Week 1 — Foundation

| Day | Task | Time |
|-----|------|------|
| **1** | Test live site + demo store on mobile. Fix anything broken. Set `CONTACT_EMAIL` on Render. | 1 hr |
| **2** | Update LinkedIn/Facebook/Instagram bio with demo link. Pin best post. | 30 min |
| **3** | Build list of 25 prospects in `lead-tracker.csv` (promo distributors on LinkedIn) | 1 hr |
| **4** | Send 10 LinkedIn connection requests + note (Template A) | 45 min |
| **5** | Send 10 cold emails (Template B). Post LinkedIn Post #1. | 1 hr |
| **6** | Follow up 5 people from Day 4–5. Add 25 more names to tracker. | 45 min |
| **7** | Rest OR catch up. Review: how many replies? | 15 min |

**Week 1 target:** 20 outreaches, 2–3 replies, 1 demo booked
