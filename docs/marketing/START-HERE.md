## If you're a beginner — do this today (60 minutes)

### Step 1 (10 min) — Test your funnel
1. Open https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/store?tenant=demo on your phone
2. Submit a test on the **Request free pilot** form at https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/#get-started
3. Check leads at `GET /api/demo-requests` (or read `data/demo_requests.jsonl` on server)

### Step 2 (15 min) — Update your profiles
Paste this in LinkedIn / Facebook / Instagram bio:

```
White-label sourcing platform for promo distributors |
Branded quote stores + AI CRM | Try demo ↓
https://ai-procurement-os.onrender.com/store?tenant=demo
```

### Step 3 (20 min) — Send 5 messages
Open `outreach-templates.md` → use **Template B** (cold email) or **Template A** (LinkedIn).  
Log sends in `lead-tracker.csv`.

### Step 4 (15 min) — Post once
Copy **LinkedIn Post #1** from `linkedin-posts.md` and publish.
