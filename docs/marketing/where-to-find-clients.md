# Where to Find Your First Clients

**Ideal customer:** Promotional product distributor, marketing agency, or corporate gifting company with 1–20 employees, based in US/Canada/UK, actively sourcing from overseas factories.

---

## Free places to find prospects (start here)

### LinkedIn (best for B2B)

**Search terms:**
- "promotional products" + "owner" OR "founder" OR "president"
- "corporate gifts" + "distributor"
- "branded merchandise" + "sales"
- "PPAI" OR "ASI" (industry associations — members are your buyers)

**How to search:**
1. LinkedIn → Search → People
2. Keywords: `promotional products distributor`
3. Filter: Company size 1–50
4. Save 10 profiles/day to your tracker

**Groups to join:**
- Promotional Products Professionals
- PPAI Member Community (if accessible)
- ASI Central Network
- Marketing Agency Owners
- B2B Sales & Lead Generation

---

### Facebook (best for communities)

**Groups to search and join:**
- Promotional Products Network
- Promo Products Distributors
- ASI Distributors
- Corporate Gifting Professionals
- Event Planners & Swag
- Small Business Marketing Tips

**How to use groups (without getting banned):**
1. Lurk 3 days — read what people ask
2. Answer 2–3 questions helpfully (no links)
3. On day 4+, share demo link only when directly relevant
4. Use Template E from `outreach-templates.md`

---

### Reddit (low volume, high intent)

**Subreddits:**
- r/smallbusiness
- r/Entrepreneur
- r/marketing
- r/ecommerce

**Don't spam.** Answer threads like:
- "How do you manage supplier relationships?"
- "CRM for B2B sales?"
- "Sourcing products from Alibaba for clients?"

Mention you built a tool, link demo store once.

## Who NOT to waste time on

| Skip | Why |
|------|-----|
| 4imprint, Vistaprint, large nationals | They have internal tech teams |
| Pure print shops (no sourcing) | Wrong workflow |
| Companies with 500+ employees | Long procurement cycles |
| Anyone who says "we use Salesforce for everything" | Hard to displace early |

---

## Building your first 50-name list (60-minute exercise)

1. **LinkedIn** — 20 promo distributor owners (company size 1–50)
2. **Google Maps** — 10 local promo shops in your city + 2 nearby cities
3. **Facebook groups** — 10 people who posted sourcing questions this week
4. **Instagram** — 10 accounts posting custom merch for clients

For each person, record in `lead-tracker.csv`:
```
name, company, email_or_linkedin, source, date_added, status
```
